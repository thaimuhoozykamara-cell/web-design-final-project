THE SIERRA LEONE FOOTBALL ASSOCIATION

The Sierra Leone Football Association (SLFA) governs football in Sierra Leone, overseeing national teams, leagues, and tournaments. Established in 1960, it's a member of CAF and FIFA, allowing participation in international competitions. The Leone Stars have had successes, including Africa Cup of Nations qualifications, but face challenges like government interference and infrastructure limitations. The SLFA focuses on youth development and grassroots programs.
